# Project Overview — Evelin Elizabeth VP | Content Writer Portfolio

---

## 1. Project Summary

A creative, editorial-style personal portfolio website for **Evelin Elizabeth VP** — copywriter and content writer at Iravata Technologies. The site is designed to reflect her voice-first philosophy: *"Words have always been more than words to me."*

Built with **Next.js**, **React**, and **Tailwind CSS**, the portfolio functions as both a professional showcase and a living expression of her creative identity — blending storytelling with strategy, and aesthetics with clarity.

---

## 2. Product Goal

To establish a compelling digital presence that communicates Evelin's value as a writer, storyteller, and brand voice specialist. The site should feel like her work — emotionally resonant, thoughtfully crafted, and impossible to forget.

**Primary outcomes:**
- Attract brand clients, agencies, and creative studios seeking a content writer
- Showcase the breadth and depth of her work across formats and industries
- Serve as a living portfolio that replaces the need for a traditional resume

## 6. Design Direction

Inspired by the UI reference provided (editorial, magazine-style content creator portfolio):

| Attribute | Direction |
|---|---|
| **Aesthetic** | Editorial / magazine — bold type, generous whitespace, strong visual hierarchy |
| **Typography** | Large expressive display font for headings; clean serif or humanist sans for body |
| **Color Palette** | Warm neutrals (cream, warm white) with one strong accent (deep ink, terracotta, or moss) |
| **Layout** | Asymmetric, scroll-driven, grid-breaking elements — not a standard card grid |
| **Imagery** | Creator-style photos, device mockups for work samples, brand logos |
| **Motion** | Scroll-triggered text reveals, subtle fade-ins, hover states on work cards |
| **Tone** | Human, warm, confident — the site should *feel* like her voice |

## 8. Feature Scope

### ✅ In Scope (Initial Release)
- All core pages: Hero, About, Brands, Work Portfolio, Experience, Contact
- Fully responsive (mobile-first: phone, tablet, desktop)
- Scroll animations and micro-interactions
- Static content — no CMS required at launch

### 🔜 Out of Scope (Future Phases)
